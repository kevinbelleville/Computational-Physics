Source: http://www.sidc.be/silso/, WDC-SILSO, Royal Observatory of Belgium, Brussels.
Start date: 01/01/1966

Resolution: 1 month
Columns:
#1 - fractional date [year],
#2 - monthly mean total sunspot number,
